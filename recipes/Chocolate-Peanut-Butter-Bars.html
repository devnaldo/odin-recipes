Chocolate Peanut Butter Bars

Home

Description

These bars are a classic blend of peanut butter and chocolate. Easy to make, and quick to satisfy!

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 2 ¾ cups confectioners' sugar

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, stir together graham cracker crumbs, confectioners' sugar, peanut butter and melted butter.
  2. Press firmly into the bottom of a 9x13 inch pan. Melt chocolate chips over a double boiler or in the microwave, stirring occasionally.
  3. Spread melted chocolate over the crumb crust. Chill for about 5 minutes, then cut into bars before the chocolate is completely set, then chill until ready to serve.
